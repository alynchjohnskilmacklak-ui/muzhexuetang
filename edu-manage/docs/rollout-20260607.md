### Reusable Knowledge for Future Sessions
- **Mobile Button Layering**: When adding fixed action bars on mobile, always use `zIndex: 400+` and `bottom: calc(60px + env(safe-area-inset-bottom))` to clear the global bottom tab bar.
- **Schema Parity**: Ensure `schema.pg.prisma` and `schema.prisma` are identical for PostgreSQL deployments to avoid build mismatches.
- **Brand Consistency**: Stick to `#E8784A` (Orange) for primary actions and `#5a4e3a` (Warm Gray) for body text.
